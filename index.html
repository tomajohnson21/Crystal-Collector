Crystal Collector

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on crystals you will add a specific amount to your score.

You win by matching your score to the given random number. You lose if you go over the given number.

The value of each crystal is unknown until you click on it.

When you start a new game, the values of the crystals change.

You may start a new game during the middle of a game by hitting the reset button, however, this will count as a loss.
